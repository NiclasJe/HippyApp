About Hippy

Hippy is a social and interactive music application that uses Spotifyâ€™s SDK to create a unique challenge for music enthusiasts. The app centers around arranging songs on a timeline in the correct chronological order, with optional settings for guessing details like release years or artists.

Hippy offers two session modes: Local and Public. In Local sessions, participants engage together on a single device, taking turns to answer challenges and arrange songs on the timeline. This mode is perfect for casual, in-person gatherings where only one participant has the app downloaded.

In Public sessions, participants join a shared lobby using a session code. They participate under customizable rules, like hijacking. Hijacking allows participants to interrupt an active turn, providing them with an opportunity to answer questions about the currently playing song and potentially earn additional points if they answer correctly.

The Hippy app transforms music into an engaging and competitive experience. Whether enjoyed with friends or other music lovers, Hippy is designed to entertain and connect users through the power of music.
