Privacy Policy for Hippy

Your privacy is important to us! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide when using Hippy.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect or store any personal information such as names, email addresses, or other identifiable data. The only information stored on our servers is data related to game state, including:

  • Player nicknames (a new name is chosen for each game session)
  • Game statistics and game progress (e.g., scores, game state)
  • Information about minimum app version requirements and current server time

This data is used solely to support game functionality and enhance your gaming experience.

2. Crash Analytics

To improve app performance and stability, we collect anonymous crash analytics data. This data helps us identify and fix issues in the app to enhance the user experience. No personal information is collected through crash analytics.

3. How We Use Collected Data

The data we collect is used only to:

  • Enable game features, such as saving game progress and settings.
  • Ensure your app version is up-to-date.
  • Improve the game based on general usage data.
  • Resolve app crashes and improve stability.

We do not sell, share, or otherwise make your data available to third parties.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the data stored on our servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

5. Children and User Data

Our app is accessible to children under 13; however, the app is not primarily designed for users below the age of 13. We do not collect personal data or sensitive information from any users, including children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be published here, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
